That’s why we’re upfront about the fact that customs charges may apply, depending on your country’s rules. The great news? These charges are pretty standard and straightforward.

Here’s what you can expect:

  • Know Your Country’s Rules: Some countries may charge VAT, import duties, or similar fees. It’s always a good idea to check your country’s import policies.
